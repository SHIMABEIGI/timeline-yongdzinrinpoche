This interactive and evolving timeline offers a carefully curated introduction to the landmark journey of His Eminence Yongdzin Lopön Tenzin Namdak Rinpoche — a revered master and one of the most influential figures in the Yungdrung Bön tradition. Tracing the arc of his spiritual path illuminates the key milestones of a life dedicated to wisdom, service, and the preservation of one of the world’s oldest spiritual lineages. This timeline is part of a broader initiative conceived and built by Shima Beigi, under the guidance of Khenchen Tenpa Yungdrung Rinpoche, the abbot of Triten Norbutse Monastery. The information has been carefully reviewed by Khenchen Rinpoche to ensure authenticity and integrity.
