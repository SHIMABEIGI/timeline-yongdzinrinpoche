This interactive timeline honors the life and legacy of His Eminence Yongdzin Lopön Tenzin Namdak Rinpoche—one of the great masters of the Yungdrung Bön tradition. It highlights key milestones of a life devoted to wisdom, service, and preserving an ancient spiritual lineage. Created for a digital audience, developed by Shima Beigi under the guidance of Khenchen Tenpa Yungdrung Rinpoche, and reviewed for authenticity, this living timeline invites you to reflect, learn, and draw inspiration from a master whose life itself was a profound teaching.
